Building the Workforce of Tomorrow: Empowering Young Talent in Rail

Join a hands-on session exploring the requirements of the future railway workforce and how they match the skills and career aspirations of young people. Engage in discussions with current students and recent graduates, and help shape the future of the railway industry. Dr Holly Foss and a Teaching Fellow will lead the way, joined by a selection of current students.
