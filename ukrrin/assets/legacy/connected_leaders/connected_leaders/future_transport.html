Railways of Tomorrow: Navigating the Future Transport Ecosystem

Join a foresight session exploring the challenges and opportunities for the railway sector in the future. Apply horizon scanning techniques and take a holistic systems approach to the dynamics between technical and strategic aspects of railway planning and operations. Dr Marcelo Blumenfeld and Dr Jenny Illingsworth will guide you in imagining the railways of tomorrow.
