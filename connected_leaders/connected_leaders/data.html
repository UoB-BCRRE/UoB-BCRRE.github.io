Data-Driven Rail: Securing the Future with Integration and Cybersecurity

Discover the opportunities and benefits of integrated data, the Internet of Things, blockchains, and machine learning in the railway system. Learn about the importance of a cyber-secure environment, and how Project STUB is using blockchain technology to enhance smart ticketing and revenue collection. Join Dr Joe Preece and Dr John Easton as they make the case for cybersecurity and help you appreciate the potential threats in this data-driven world.
