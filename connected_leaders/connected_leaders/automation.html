Electrifying Innovations: Smart Systems for the Railways

Explore how electronic and mechatronic systems are solving everyday railway issues through innovative solutions like inertial measurement units (IMUs), acoustic monitoring, and railway robotics. Learn about MoniRail, a BCRRE spinout company using IMUs, and see how the railway is advancing with the help of these cutting-edge technologies. Join Dr Edward Stewart, Dr Mani Entezami, Dr Jiaqi Ye, and Huayu Duan as they lead the way.
