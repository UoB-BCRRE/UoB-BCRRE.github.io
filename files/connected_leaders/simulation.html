Simulating Success: The Power of BRaVE

Get a hands-on demonstration of the Birmingham Railway Virtual Environment (BRaVE) and learn about its current case studies. Delegates will understand the value of simulation within the systems engineering V-curve and the impact of a whole-system design approach to railways. Clive Roberts and David Kirkwood will lead the way.
